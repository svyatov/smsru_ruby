# Code of Conduct

This project adheres to the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/),
version 2.1.

In short: be respectful, welcoming, and considerate. Harassment and other
exclusionary behavior are not tolerated.

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the maintainer at **leonid@svyatov.com**. All complaints will be
reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

The full text of the Contributor Covenant is available at the link above.
